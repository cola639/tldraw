This very good whiteboard is made by possible by a cheeky group of nerds in the London coding scene that are hacking together geometry, arrows, deli sandwiches, and huppiness.
We aim to hack and craft our way through the unexplored map edges of the Sea of Coding.
This website was coded entirely in Dreamberd.
